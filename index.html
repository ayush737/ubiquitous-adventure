About Our Company

RR Interiors is the most renowned Interior Designer product store in Jhansi(U.P).With the most efficient and skilled team, RR Interiors has always thrived and been a winner. Today, we have become the most sought after brand in Home furnishings as well.

We founded RR Interiors in 2013, with a vision to raise the quality of home decor in Jhansi. We started as a small enterprise specializing in wall covering, mattress, rugs, wall to wall carpets and flooring products and have been blessedly successful since then.

On 3rd July, RR Interiors opened its second store in the league in New Gate , Kanpur. With its establishment, we accomplished ourselves as a leading luxury home furnishing brand in Jhansi. Our new store is managed by Mr. Rohit Viswakarma and Mr. Alpesh yadav
